Problems With the Old Site

❌ No Online Presence

No website at all — customers had to find Darcy through word of mouth or FlightCircle. Losing leads to competitors with professional sites.

❌ No Booking System

No way for customers to book discovery flights or experiences online. Every inquiry required a phone call or walk-in.

❌ Zero SEO

Not indexed by Google. People searching "flight training Danbury CT" or "discovery flight Connecticut" never found Darcy.

❌ No Content Control

No way to update pricing, descriptions, training programs, or fleet info without a developer. Changes took days/weeks.

❌ No Credibility Signals

No testimonials, fleet details, maintenance info, or FAQ. Potential students had no way to evaluate Darcy before calling.

❌ Not Mobile-Friendly

60%+ of flight school inquiries come from mobile. Without a responsive site, most visitors bounced immediately.
