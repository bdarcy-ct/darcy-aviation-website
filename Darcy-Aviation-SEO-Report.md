## What is SEO?

**Search Engine Optimization (SEO)** is how your website gets found on Google, Bing, and other search engines. When someone in your area searches for "flight training Danbury CT" or "scenic flights Connecticut," good SEO is what puts your site at the top of those results — for free.

Unlike paid ads, SEO builds **long-term organic traffic** that keeps growing over time.

## What We've Built Into Your Site

### 1. Page-Level Meta Tags (Every Page)

Every page on your site has custom **title tags** and **meta descriptions** — these are what Google shows in search results.

Each training sub-page (PPL, Instrument, Commercial, Multi-Engine, Discovery, Simulator) also has its own SEO title and description.

**Why it matters:** Google uses these to decide what your page is about and where to rank it.

### 2. Open Graph & Social Media Tags

Every page includes **Open Graph (OG)** and **Twitter Card** meta tags. This means when someone shares your site on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, or Twitter, it shows:

- A nice preview image
- The correct title
- A compelling description

Instead of a blank link, people see a professional, branded preview — which gets more clicks.

### 3. Structured Data (Schema.org)

Your **FAQ page** includes **FAQPage structured data** — a special code format that Google understands. This can trigger **rich results** in Google search, where your FAQ questions appear directly in the search results with expandable answers.

This gives your listing more visual space on Google and increases click-through rates significantly.

### 4. Sitemap & Robots.txt

**Sitemap (sitemap.xml):**
A file that lists every page on your site for Google to find and index. Your sitemap includes all 16+ pages with priority rankings:

- **Priority 1.0:** Homepage
- **Priority 0.9:** Experiences, Training
- **Priority 0.8:** Individual training pages, Fleet, Maintenance
- **Priority 0.7:** About, FAQ, Contact

**Robots.txt:**
Tells search engines they're welcome to crawl your entire site. No pages are blocked.

**⚠️ Action Needed:** The sitemap currently references the old Railway URL. Update it to use `darcyaviation.com` for best results (instructions below).

### 5. Geo-Location Tags

Your site includes geographic meta tags that help with **local search**:

- **Region:** US-CT (Connecticut)
- **City:** Danbury
- **Coordinates:** 41.3714, -73.4822 (KDXR airport)
- **ICBM tag:** Exact coordinates for geo-search engines

This helps Google understand you're a **local business** and show you in "near me" searches and Google Maps results.

### 6. Performance & Technical SEO

- **Code splitting** — Pages load only what they need (faster load times)
- **Lazy loading** — Images and components load as you scroll
- **Immutable asset caching** — Returning visitors load instantly
- **Error boundaries** — Site never shows a blank white screen
- **Mobile responsive** — Works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops
- **HTTPS/SSL** — Secure connection (Google ranks secure sites higher)
- **Clean URLs** — `/training/ppl` not `/page?id=123`

### 7. Content SEO

- **Keyword-rich headings** on every page (flight training, Danbury, Connecticut, KDXR)
- **Natural keyword usage** throughout page content
- **Internal linking** between related pages (training → experiences → contact)
- **Image alt tags** on logos and key images
- **Fast-loading pages** (under 2 seconds)

## Your CMS SEO Settings

You can edit SEO settings for every page directly from your admin panel:

1. Go to **darcyaviation.com/admin**
2. Log in with your credentials
3. Click **SEO Settings** in the sidebar
4. For each page, you can edit:
   - **Page Title** — what shows in the browser tab and Google results
   - **Meta Description** — the summary text under your title in Google
   - **OG Title** — title for social media shares
   - **OG Description** — description for social media shares
   - **OG Image** — preview image for social media shares

**Best Practices for Titles:**
- Keep under 60 characters
- Include your main keyword + location
- Example: "Private Pilot Training — Danbury CT — Darcy Aviation"

**Best Practices for Descriptions:**
- Keep under 155 characters
- Include a call to action
- Example: "Earn your private pilot license at KDXR with experienced instructors. Discovery flights from $279. Call (203) 617-0645."
